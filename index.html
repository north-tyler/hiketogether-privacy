Privacy Policy

HikeTogether
Last Updated: January 6, 2026

Data Collection

HikeTogether collects the following data to provide the app's functionality:

  • Step count and walking/running distance from HealthKit (with your permission)
  • Adventure progress and milestone achievements
  • User profile information (name, avatar)
  • Friend connections and shared adventure data
  • App preferences and notification settings

Data Storage & Sharing

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (a secure cloud database service). We:

  • Store adventure and profile data on secure servers to enable friend features
  • Never sell your data to third parties
  • Only share your progress with friends you explicitly connect with
  • Do not track your real-time location
  • Do not use advertising or third-party analytics

HealthKit Data

Health data is accessed only with your explicit permission. Your raw HealthKit data never leaves your device - only aggregated step counts and distances are synced to enable adventure progress tracking.

Push Notifications

With your permission, we send notifications for:

  • Milestone achievements during your adventures
  • Optional daily reminders to log your steps

You can disable notifications anytime in Settings or iOS Settings.

Friend Connections

When you add friends, they can see:

  • Your shared adventure progress
  • Your position on adventure maps
  • Your total distance and milestones

Friends cannot see your actual location, step data, or health information.

Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • All data transmission uses encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Database access requires authentication
  • Row-level security protects your adventure data

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Revoke HealthKit access anytime in iOS Settings
  • Disable notifications in app Settings
  • Remove friends to stop sharing progress
  • Reset adventures to clear progress data
  • Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Contact us to request account and data deletion

Children's Privacy

HikeTogether is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
